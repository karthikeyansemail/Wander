Customer app features

  • Quick orders

    For items like grocery or hyper local delivery users can order by voice message, photo of grocery list, or medical prescription or a typed list of items.

  • Subscription orders

    For frequent deliverable items like food, milk, news paper etc.. a calender based flexible weekly, monthly, yearly subscription option available.

  • Delivery slots

    Users can select the exact date time slot that when the product needs to be delivered to them, provided if such option given in merchant app.

  • Booking slots

    For immediate delivery items like food, users will be allowed to order on specific timings like lunch, dinner and display next available timing slots to book.

  • Google Maps

    Adding address during checkout is as easy like tapping the location on maps. Delivery address will be automatically taken and store for future orders.

  • Notifications

    Customers will get automatic SMS & Android and iOS push notifications about every stage of order status like processing, canceled, returned, delivered.

Merchant App features

  • Manage Orders

    Orders are sorted with separated tabs new, processing, delivered, canceled, returned, return initiated subscription orders etc.. you can order details and assign delivery person appropriately.

  • Manage Products

    Products details and images can be added, edited and managed easily including marking no stock items from your merchant app and which disallows to user to order no stock items.

  • Manage Categories

    Main categories to be displayed in the main home screen along with category images can be added and sub categories to be displayed as tabs can be easily managed from your merchant application.

  • Manage Delivery

    you can enable disable and control delivery slots, booking slots and delivery charges standard, fast delivery options, cancellation, returns and distance fee charges based on KM etc..

  • Shop Appearance

    Customize your app theme color and banners product list methods like grid and list etc.. payment methods Pay on delivery or Google pay online payment and setup payment charges if any.

  • Route Maps

    Get pin point location of your customers along with distance details right in your order list to sort and filter your orders based on distance to manage delivery start drive directions.

  • Sales Tracking

    Monitor your online sales progress using our sales tracking feature which provides daily, weekly, monthly and yearly graphical charts on your total sales with different type of orders.

  • Delivery Boy App

    We will provide you a dedicated mobile app for your delivery professionals to manage their delivery with order details and drive directions feature with Google maps embedded.
